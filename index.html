Snowbound Vow

Deedra (Aes Sedai) and Ray (her Warder) stand against Trollocs in the snow—channeling light and steel together.

Two hearts, one bond. Their power and blades keep the night at bay.

Flow: Begin → Move to dodge → Channel (Space) and Slash (F) to defeat Trollocs → Survive waves → Romantic ending.

How to Play

  • Move with Arrow Keys or A/D; dodge Trolloc charges.
  • Channel with Space (ranged blast, costs channel energy). Slash with F (Ray’s strike, short range).
  • Survive waves of Trollocs; defeat them all. Hearts are shared between Deedra and Ray.
  • Channel energy refills slowly. Keep your positioning cel-sharp to avoid hits.
  • Reach the final wave to unlock their romantic ending beneath the snow.
  • Use the light/dark toggle to pick your vibe; SFX toggle for subtle chimes.

Wheel of Time vibe: Aes Sedai channeling flare, Warder blade arcs, Trolloc horned silhouettes.

Holiday vibe: Red/green/gold/white cel-shaded palette, snowfall, and heart sparks.
