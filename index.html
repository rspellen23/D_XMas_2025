How to Play

  • Click a tile next to the empty space to slide it. Rearrange tiles to form an unbroken path from Deedra (left) to Ray (right).
  • Fewer slides = higher score. Time also counts.
  • Tiles include straights, curves, tees, and crosses. The empty space is dark.
  • Complete the connection to unlock their ending beneath the snow.
  • After the path, align the three rings: use the Rotate Outer/Middle/Inner buttons until all rings are gold and spokes line up; a green glow appears when they are correct, then click “See the Vow.”
  • Use the light/dark toggle for vibe; SFX toggle for chimes.
