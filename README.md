# D_XMas_2025 – Wheel of Christmas Time

Open `index.html` in a browser to play a Christmas/Wheel of Time themed game built for Deedra. Spin the Wheel of Gifts, sprint to collect Ajah-colored presents while dodging Trolloc snowballs, and answer a quick prophecy quiz to earn more hearts and points. After a few runs, a short ending appears—Ray’s holiday wish to Deedra.

Tech: vanilla HTML/CSS/JS (Canvas). Works offline; no build step required.
